Our purpose

Traffic Flow-and-Go is a schedule builder designed to use the recent development of hybrid work to reduce urban traffic congestion during the standard workweek

How This Works

Using a list of employees in a city, including their home location, work location, and their mandatory in-person workdays, this website finds an optimal balance of in-person and remote workdays for each worker. This balance is then entered into a schedule, that, if followed, will reduce overall traffic congestion. Employees can be added or removed from the list. Also, employee information in the list can be edited.

The Problem

Since the quality of municipal transit systems varies from city to city and the quality of these systems is often low, many workers are forced to drive if they want a somewhat reasonable commute time. This results in a greater amount of cars on the road at a given time and a greater chance of congestion. Traffic congestion is a problem that both reduces worker well-being and increases the amount of carbon a vehicle emits during a drive.
